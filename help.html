How do I use the tool?

  1. Enter the ID from your softres.it URL.
  2. Enter the number of points you want to increment by (this actually doesn't matter unless you're uploading a .csv, but the form requires a number either way).
  3. If you already have a .csv from a previous raid, click the "Browse" button next to "Upload previous CSV", and select the .csv file that you generated last time you used this tool.
  4. Click "Generate CSV" and wait for it to process.
  5. Once processing completes, click the download link.
